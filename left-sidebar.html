A Cooperative Approach to Agribusiness Success

Situated in the fertile heart of Remo North LGA, Ogun State, our initiative is a dynamic partnership between three visionary entities.

This collaboration, under the capable leadership of Evangelist Kolade Segun‑Okeowo, is built on a shared commitment to transforming agribusiness into a sustainable, profitable, and community‑driven enterprise. Our partners are:

  • Ilusanmi Cooperative
  • Zionstones Farms
  • Script to Screen Cooperatives

Our Business Model

At the core of our operations is the Ilusanmi Cooperative’s innovative structure, which empowers individuals to own and profit from oil palm farmland without the burden of daily management. We professionally manage the land from cultivation to harvest, ensuring maximum yield, and then market and sell the produce strategically to deliver consistent returns to our members. This model strengthens the local economy by creating jobs, fostering agricultural expertise, and promoting responsible land use.

Cultivating Prosperity Together

By joining forces with Zionstones Farms and Script to Screen Cooperatives, we combine agricultural expertise, strategic management, and innovative outreach to create an agribusiness model that delivers tangible results. Together, we are cultivating more than crops — we are cultivating prosperity, security, and long‑term value for every member.
