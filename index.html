Learn About Investing

Farmland Investment

Invest in oil palm farmland with our "Operation Buy and Earn (OBE)" model, generating income while your asset appreciates in value.

Cooperative Benefits

Benefit from our collective resources, including access to grants, cheaper labor, bulk harvesting capabilities, and joint security for your investment.

Full Farm Management

We offer end-to-end services, including professional farmland cultivation and management, and handle the sale of all oil palm produce for you.

A Cooperative Approach to Agribusiness Success

Located in the Remo North LGA of Ogun State, our project is a collaboration between the Ilusanmi Cooperative, Zionstones Farms, and Script to Screen Cooperatives, chaired by Evangelist Kolade Segun-Okeowo.

Our Business Model

Ilusanmi operates as a cooperative, allowing individuals to own and profit from oil palm farmland. We manage the land, cultivate the crop, and sell the produce, providing a return on your investment through a proven, community-focused model.

Why Invest With Us?

By joining our cooperative, you gain access to economies of scale that individual farmers cannot. Cheaper labor, professional management, bulk harvesting, and robust security ensure your investment is both safe and profitable.
