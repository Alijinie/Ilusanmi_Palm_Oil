Leadership and Vision Behind Ilusanmi Oil Palm Farms

Meet the team and the structure guiding our success.

A Visionary Leader

At the helm of the Ilusanmi Cooperative Multi‑Purpose Society Limited is Evangelist Kolade Segun‑Okeowo, a visionary leader whose passion for sustainable agriculture and community empowerment has shaped the success of the Ilusanmi Oil Palm Farms. As the Chairman of the Cooperative and the initiator of the oil palm project, Evangelist Segun‑Okeowo brings together a wealth of experience in leadership, strategic planning, and enterprise development. His dual role as Managing Director/Chief Executive Officer of Zionstones Media further reflects his versatility and commitment to excellence across multiple sectors.

The Cooperative Structure

The Ilusanmi Oil Palm Farms operate under the management of the Ilusanmi Cooperative Multi‑Purpose Society Limited, a community‑driven organization dedicated to creating wealth and opportunities through shared agricultural investment. This cooperative model ensures that members benefit from collective ownership, professional farm management, and economies of scale that maximize productivity and profitability.

To ensure transparency, efficiency, and optimal returns, all investments are managed through Zionstones Media, leveraging its robust administrative systems and business acumen. This structure allows investors to participate in a thriving agricultural venture without the operational challenges of running a farm, while still enjoying consistent updates, clear profit‑sharing arrangements, and the security of professional oversight.

A Synergy for Success

Under Evangelist Segun‑Okeowo’s leadership, the Ilusanmi Oil Palm Farms have become more than just a business—they represent a sustainable pathway to economic growth, rural development, and food security. By combining the cooperative’s grassroots strength with Zionstones Media’s management expertise, the project delivers measurable value to its members, supports local employment, and contributes to the revitalization of Nigeria’s agricultural sector. This synergy of vision, structure, and execution positions Ilusanmi Oil Palm Farms as a model for profitable, community‑focused agribusiness in the region.
